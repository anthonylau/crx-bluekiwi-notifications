The unofficial blueKiwi extension for Google Chrome

This is the repository for the unofficial blueKiwi extension for Google Chrome. You can download the latest stable release from: https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/bluekiwi-notifier/nmokaddnjebadokcmjfbinphpfkenghp?utm_source=chrome-ntp-icon
If you wish to contribute, please fork the repository

What does it do?

It's adds a button in chrome, after click it will show the notifications of a platform of choice. It also features:

  • A notification counter in your browser will show when new notifications have been send to you
  • Notification popups without Chrome being open
  • One click redirection to any notification from the blueKiwi notifier to post in blueKiwi
  • A convenient configuration page in which you can supply your instance name, interval and visibility of notifications
  • A home button, to direct you to the homepage of the configured instance
  • blueKiwi notifier will utilize the connection of your existing instance connections for authentication

Support or Contact

No. It's provided for free and as is, bugs will be fixed but it has no official support from blueKiwi. Neither can customers put in tickets in the support desk of Atos, or blueKiwi. You as user as responsible for installation in the chome browser. Given the small nature of the extension, problems will be minimal.

How do I install it?

  1. Go to the URL or search for BK in the chrome app store
  2. Install it
  3. supply the instance of your choice
  4. That's it..
