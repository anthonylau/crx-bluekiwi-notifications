How to find your space management settings

Step 1

Open up blueKiwi and navigate to your profile page by clicking on your name in the "Home" tab

Step 2

Click on the "Settings" option in the left hand navigation panel

Step 3

Click on the "Spaces" tab in the settings page

Step 4

Copy the URL at the top and paste it into the blueKiwi Extension Options page. It should look something like:
https://zen.domain.net/user/in/Joe_Bloggs/settings/spaces

Step 5
Click Save
